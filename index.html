Conversation Christ

Welcome to Conversation Christ. This is where you can engage in conversations about anything and everything, with Christ at the center of it all. Nothing is off-limits. Honesty is what Jesus taught so let's share our experiences and ask tough questions.

  • Let's go

Believers and Non-Believers Welcome

This is a place for Christians, atheists, agnostics and people of all faiths. Jesus loves everyone, regardless of their background or what part of the world they come from. He wishes for everyone to accept Him as their Lord and Saviour.

  • Learn more

Who We Are

Conversation Christ was created by a former atheist and addict. He wanted to make a refuge for genuine discussions about faith and life's many struggles. If you find it difficult to relate with most people then you will fit in perfectly here.

  • Keep reading

Got Questions?

Good! Asking difficult questions and sharing personal testimonies is not just encouraged but it's the reason this site was created. If you have questions about Christianity, the Bible or anything else then you can ask them here without fear of being judged.
